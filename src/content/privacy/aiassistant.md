# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** 15th January 2025 · **Version 1.0**

utsab jetani ("We", "Us", "Our") operates the AIAssistant mobile application ("Application").

## 1. Information Processing
* **API Integration:** This application connects directly to Google Gemini APIs to process your queries. Your queries are sent securely to Google's servers for text generation and are subject to Google's API Privacy Terms.
* **On-Device Data:** Conversation histories and session configurations are stored entirely on your local device. We do not store your dialogues on our servers.

## 2. API Keys
If you use your own API key to access services, the key is encrypted and stored locally on your device's secure keychain.
