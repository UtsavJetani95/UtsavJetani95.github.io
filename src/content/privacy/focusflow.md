# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** 15th January 2025 · **Version 1.0**

utsab jetani ("We", "Us", "Our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices for the FocusFlow mobile application ("Application").

## 1. Information Collection and Use
FocusFlow is designed as an offline-first productivity and Pomodoro timer application. 
* **Personal Data:** We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (such as your name, email, or telephone number).
* **Usage Data:** All productivity session data, task lists, and timer settings are stored locally on your device and are never sent to external servers.

## 2. Third-Party Services
We may use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous, aggregated diagnostic data (such as app crash logs and general feature usage) to improve application stability and experience. These third-party services are governed by their own privacy policies.

## 3. Data Deletion
Since all user data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all data associated with the Application at any time by clearing the application cache or uninstalling the app.
