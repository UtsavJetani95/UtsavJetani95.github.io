Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 15th January 2025 · Version 1.0

utsab jetani ("We", "Us", "Our") operates the SpendWise mobile application ("Application"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

1. Zero Data Collection Philosophy

SpendWise is an offline-first expense tracker.

  • Financial Data: All financial records, expense logs, budget targets, and category structures are stored locally on your device. We have no access to your financial data.
  • No Server Sync: The app does not sync your data to our servers. Your data remains entirely in your control.

    • 2. Cloud Backup

    If you opt to backup your data via Google Drive or iCloud, the backup files are stored securely in your private cloud storage account. We do not have access to these files or accounts.
